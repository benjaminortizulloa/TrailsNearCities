The CDC recently reported that "the number of states in which at least 35% of residents have obesity has nearly doubled since 2018." Today, there are 16 states that reach this 35% threshold. 1

Minorities suffer even more than the general population with nearly half the Black population and 45% of the Latinx population having obesity. 2 More minority families shouldered the economic brunt of the pandemic because they represent a greater share of the low-wage or essential workforce. This suggests we search for an equitable solution to obesity that takes financial constraints into account.

Luckily, congress passed the National Trails System Act in 1968 3 which honors trails that are open to the public, represents their region, and supports a diverse community.
